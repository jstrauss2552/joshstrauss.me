About me

Josh Strauss is a mission-driven builder and founder based in Austin, TX & Miami, FL. He is the founder & CEO of GumGauge and a student at the University of Austin (UATX), shipping software end to end across native apps and private-capital fintech.

The reason behind all of it is simple: he wants to help people and leave the world better than he found it. That thread runs from nine years of youth human-rights advocacy with B’nai B’rith International, from delegations to the UN in Geneva and UNESCO in Paris to leading a youth mission to Colombia, through growing up working events for the family production studio, to the ventures he builds today as a student at UATX, where he studies economics, politics, and history. Getting there meant leaving Northeastern after one semester: a deliberate bet on an unaccredited school and the like-minded people in it, the kind who constantly and willingly challenge themselves to push the limits of what it means to be an active member of society.
